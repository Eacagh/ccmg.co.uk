Welcome to the Clarendon Chamber Music Group

Professional chamber music coaching for adult string players — relaxed, friendly, and deeply inspiring.

Whether you already play - or would like to play - in a quartet, trio, or other ensemble, CCMG offers expert coaching designed to help you deepen your musicianship, refine ensemble skills, and experience the joy of shared music-making.

Our sessions are open to dedicated amateurs of all levels, whether individuals looking to join or form a group, or existing groups. Each session is shaped around a group’s individual goals — from confident communication to expressive interpretation — in a warm and encouraging environment.
