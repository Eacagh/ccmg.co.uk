Our Coaches

Meet our founders and coaches — experienced professional musicians dedicated to helping others discover the joy and depth of chamber music.

Every session at CCMG is guided by the belief that great chamber music comes from attentive listening, mutual trust, and an open, collaborative spirit. Our approach blends musical insight with a supportive, good-humoured atmosphere that encourages genuine growth.

Enrico Alvares

A highly experienced violinist, chamber musician, and coach, Enrico studied with the Amadeus Quartet and later worked closely with Sir Neville Marriner and the Academy of St Martin in the Fields. He now devotes his time to teaching, performing, and helping others cultivate expressive and intelligent ensemble playing.

Stephanie Waite

A violinist and teacher with wide-ranging experience in orchestral, chamber, and educational settings, Stephanie brings warmth, clarity, and deep musical understanding to her coaching. She has a special gift for helping players build confidence and connection through sound.
